Method Notes

  • Simple-span demand model with truck envelope plus lane load and impact.
  • Flexural capacity uses effective prestress force and reduced steel areas for deterioration.
  • Shear capacity uses concrete plus stirrup contribution with independent stirrup loss.
  • Results report baseline (0% loss) and deteriorated case in one run.
