CMP Culvert Load Rating

Circular corrugated metal pipe culvert rating over variable fill heights with baseline vs deteriorated checks.

Method Notes

  • Runs fill-height sweep in one analysis pass and reports inventory/operating RFs by fill depth.
  • Evaluates ring compression, bending, buckling, seam, and serviceability deflection limit states.
  • Deterioration explicitly models steel wall-thickness loss (uniform or segmented crown/springline/invert).
  • Optional reinforced/prestressed composite contributions include explicit rebar and prestress section loss.
